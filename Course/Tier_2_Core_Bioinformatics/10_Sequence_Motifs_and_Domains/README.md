# 10. Sequence Motifs and Domains

**Tier 2: Core Bioinformatics**

Discovering and characterizing functional patterns in biological sequences. Builds Position Weight Matrices from scratch, creates sequence logos, and scans sequences against major protein domain databases.

## Topics Covered

- Biological significance of sequence motifs: TF binding sites, splice signals, PTM sites
- Constructing PWMs from aligned binding sites: PFM, PPM, PWM with pseudocounts
- Information content and sequence logos
- PROSITE patterns and regex conversion
- Protein domain databases: Pfam, InterPro, CDD
- Scanning sequences for known domains
