# 8. Nucleic Acid Structure

**Tier 2: Core Bioinformatics**

The chemistry and geometry of DNA and RNA structure, from nucleotide components to helical forms and RNA secondary structure elements. Introduces 3DNA for computational nucleic acid analysis.

## Topics Covered

- Chemical components of nucleotides: base, sugar, phosphate
- Watson-Crick base pairing and hydrogen bonds
- DNA helical forms: A-DNA, B-DNA, Z-DNA and biological significance
- Major and minor groove geometry and protein-DNA recognition
- RNA secondary structure elements: stems, loops, bulges, pseudoknots
- Introduction to 3DNA for structural analysis
