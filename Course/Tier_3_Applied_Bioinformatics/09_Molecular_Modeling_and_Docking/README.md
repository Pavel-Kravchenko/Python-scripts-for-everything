# 9. Molecular Modeling and Docking

**Tier 3: Applied Bioinformatics**

Bridging static structural data and dynamic biological reality through computational modeling. Covers force fields, energy minimization, molecular dynamics concepts, and hands-on docking with AutoDock Vina.

## Topics Covered

- Levels of modeling theory: quantum mechanics, molecular mechanics, coarse-grained models
- Force fields for biomolecules: AMBER, CHARMM, OPLS
- Energy minimization: steepest descent and conjugate gradient
- Molecular dynamics simulation concepts: integration, thermostats, periodic boundaries
- Molecular docking: rigid and flexible docking, scoring functions
- Hands-on docking with AutoDock Vina
- Virtual screening workflows
