# 3. RNA-seq Analysis

**Tier 3: Applied Bioinformatics**

Complete RNA-seq workflow from experimental design through differential expression analysis and visualization. Covers alignment, quantification, normalization, and statistical testing with DESeq2 and edgeR concepts.

## Topics Covered

- Advantages of RNA-seq over microarrays
- Experimental design: biological replicates, sequencing depth, batch effects
- Alignment with STAR and HISAT2; quantification with featureCounts, HTSeq, and salmon
- Count normalization: RPKM, FPKM, TPM, DESeq2 size factors
- Differential expression with DESeq2 and edgeR (conceptual and R integration)
- Visualization: volcano plots, MA plots, heatmaps, PCA
