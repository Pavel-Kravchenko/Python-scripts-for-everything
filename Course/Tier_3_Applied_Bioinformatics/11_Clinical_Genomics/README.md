# 11. Clinical Genomics

**Tier 3: Applied Bioinformatics**

Precision medicine and clinical interpretation of genetic variants, from ACMG/AMP classification to pharmacogenomics. Covers the full range of clinical sequencing approaches and ethical considerations in genomic medicine.

## Topics Covered

- Precision medicine: from the Human Genome Project to routine clinical sequencing
- Types of genetic testing: diagnostic, predictive, carrier, pharmacogenomic
- ACMG/AMP variant classification: pathogenic, likely pathogenic, VUS, likely benign, benign
- ClinVar and clinical variant databases
- Clinical sequencing approaches: gene panels, WES, WGS
- Pharmacogenomics: drug-gene interactions and CYP enzymes
- Ethical considerations in clinical genomics
