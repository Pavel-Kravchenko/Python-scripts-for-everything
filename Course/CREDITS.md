## Kodomo Bioinformatics Program (MSU Faculty of Bioengineering and Bioinformatics)

**Website:** [https://kodomo.fbb.msu.ru/wiki/2017](https://kodomo.fbb.msu.ru/wiki/2017)

The course materials were significantly informed by the Kodomo bioinformatics curriculum, a comprehensive program spanning 10 semesters at the Faculty of Bioengineering and Bioinformatics, Moscow State University. The original materials were in Russian and have been translated to English and adapted for self-paced learning.
